Established 2008 · Kampala, Uganda

Built here.
Proven everywhere.

Market Bolt was founded by a group of marketing professionals who believed that Ugandan brands deserved world-class activation — not a second-tier version of it. We started with conviction, a handful of clients, and fifteen years of industry experience between us.

Today we are countrywide operators. We have put brands in people's hands, in their conversations, and in their memories. From field teams in Gulu to brand experiences in Kampala, from door-to-door distribution routes to mass communication campaigns that reached millions — we have been on the ground, in the market, doing the work.

The brands that took a chance on us in 2008 are still calling. That is not luck. That is delivery.
