Privacy Policy

Market Bolt Uganda Ltd ("MBolt", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information you provide to us through our website at www.market-bolt.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you use our contact or careers forms, including your name, email address, phone number, company name, and the content of your message. We do not collect any information automatically beyond standard server logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry or application. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. We do not send newsletters or marketing communications unless you explicitly request them.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely and accessed only by relevant members of the MBolt team. We retain contact enquiry data for a maximum of 24 months. Careers application data is retained for 12 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@market-bolt.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@market-bolt.com. We will respond within 14 business days.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Fonts for typography, loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect standard web request data in connection with this. We do not use advertising trackers, retargeting pixels, or third-party analytics that identify individual users.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

Cookie Notice

This notice explains how Market Bolt Uganda Ltd uses cookies on our website at www.market-bolt.com.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies We Use

We use only essential, functional cookies necessary for the basic operation of our website:

  • Theme preference — stores your light or dark mode choice so the site remembers it on your next visit. Duration: persistent until cleared.
  • Accessibility settings — stores any accessibility preferences you set (font size, high contrast, reduced motion). Duration: persistent until cleared.
  • Cookie consent — records whether you have accepted or declined our cookie notice. Duration: 12 months.

What We Do Not Use

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, retargeting cookies, or any cookies that track your behaviour across other websites. We do not use Google Analytics or similar user-tracking services.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Deleting our functional cookies will not prevent you from using our website, but you may need to re-set your preferences. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings menus.

Third-Party Cookies

Google Fonts, used for our website typography, may set cookies related to font delivery from Google's servers. These are not within our control. For information on Google's use of data, see Google's Privacy Policy.
